Event Marketing & PR

Event Marketing and PR

We work collaboratively with you and your brand to develop effective strategies that support the promotion of your event and ensure that we maximise event growth, attendee engagement and create a stronger event presence as a whole.

We treat the development of each event Marketing and PR launch uniquely so if you don’t see something mentioned above – never hesitate to contact us as we are always happy to help.

Below are some of the services that we offer:

Event Launch Strategy

We can create tailor-made launch strategies using a variety of marketing mediums to ensure that it best serves your event. We work with you to advise the recommended methods ranging from digital through to direct marketing objectives that best suit your objectives and budget.

Event PR Campaigns

Make sure that you grow the reach of your event by creating some buzz! We ensure that your event reaches the right media outlets through press kit creation, circulation, creative media seeding techniques and live press and media coverage during events.

Event Social Media Strategy

A well-planned out social media strategy is essential for the success of your event. From the development of social media content calendars through to paid media campaigns, we can create a wide range of relevant social media options.

Marketing Collateral

Ensure that your collateral seamlessly ties in with your event through consistent messaging and branding. We are able to assist with the usual event branding such as brochures, leaflets and USB’s but we always are happy to look in to creative marketing collateral solutions.

Post-Event Reporting

We can work with you to ensure that your event KPI’s are met and transferred into tangible results that can help you going forward in your next event and future event strategy.

Live Social Media Event Coverage 

It can be complicated having an event and worrying about the social media engagement at the same time. We aim to take this off your mind and ensure that your online community are able to have a flawless online event experience with our live social media solutions.

Strategic Brand Partnerships

Create immediate positive impact on your event with an effective and meaningful collaboration that stays true to your brand and event image. Our team are fully equipped to source, propose and implement a mutually successful brand partnership that brings growth to your audience.

Talent and Influencer Engagement

As the digital world and events world overlaps we ensure that your event is associated with relevant talent and key influencers to secure authentic online amplification.
